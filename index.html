低维训练如何演化高维智能

Can Low-Dimensional Light Illuminate a High-Dimensional World?

从信息压缩、感官降维到认知升维——论文本训练的边界与智能的演化路径。

中文版 →

大型语言模型（LLM）以全人类文本为食，展现出令人惊叹的智能。然而，文本本身是对真实世界的高倍率有损压缩。核心命题：低维认知无法自主演化为高维认知，升维必须依赖新的信息源注入。

English Version →

LLMs exhibit astonishing intelligence, yet text is a high-rate lossy compression of the real world. Core thesis: low-dimensional cognition cannot autonomously evolve into high-dimensional cognition; dimensionality ascent depends on injecting new information sources.
