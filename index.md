从信息压缩、感官降维到认知升维——论文本训练的边界与智能的演化路径。

大型语言模型（LLM）以全人类文本为食，展现出令人惊叹的智能。然而，文本本身是对真实世界的高倍率有损压缩。本文论证一个核心命题：**低维认知（文本训练）无法自主演化为高维认知（真实世界理解），升维必须依赖新的信息源注入。**

## English

From Information Compression and Sensory Dimensionality Reduction to Cognitive Dimensionality Ascent — On the Boundaries of Text-Based Training and the Evolutionary Trajectory of Intelligence.

LLMs exhibit astonishing intelligence, yet text is a high-rate lossy compression of the real world. This article argues: **low-dimensional cognition cannot autonomously evolve into high-dimensional cognition; dimensionality ascent depends on injecting new information sources.**
